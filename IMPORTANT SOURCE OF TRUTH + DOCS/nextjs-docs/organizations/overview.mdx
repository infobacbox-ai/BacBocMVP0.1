---
title: Overview
description: Learn how organizations work in supastarter.
---

Organizations are a way to to share data between users. Organizations can have members with different roles and permissions.

In supastarter organizations are also handled by [better-auth](https://better-auth.com) and therefore you also have full control over the organization management and all data is stored in the database.
